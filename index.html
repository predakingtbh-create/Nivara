Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How do I schedule an emergency appointment? expand_more
Our emergency line is available 24/7. Click “Book Now” and choose “Emergency Consultation” to connect with an on‑duty vet within minutes.
Can I choose in‑person or video visit? expand_more
Yes. During booking select your preferred mode. Availability is shown in real time for both visit types.
Are the medicines authentic and verified? expand_more
All medicines are sourced from licensed pharmacies and verified by our pharmacists against your prescription.
How fast is delivery? expand_more
In major cities delivery typically arrives within 2 hours. ETA is shown at checkout based on your PIN code.
What is the adoption screening process? expand_more
The process includes an application, a virtual home check, and a conversation with a shelter coordinator to ensure a great match.
Are medical records provided? expand_more
Yes. Each pet’s vaccination and health history is included when available from the shelter or foster partner.
How do you protect my data? expand_more
We use encrypted transport (HTTPS) and follow best practices for secure storage. Sensitive files are access‑controlled and never shared without consent.
Can I delete my account and records? expand_more
Yes. You can request deletion from your profile settings. We permanently remove your data within standard compliance timelines.
